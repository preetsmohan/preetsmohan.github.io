Hey there! I'm Preeti, and I'm an Software Engineer at Google Research, working on the Speech Intelligence team in the Cerebra org. I've previously worked as a Software Engineer at Microsoft on the Cortana team and Wellsaid Labs as a Deep Learning Engineer. I got my MS in Computational Linguistics from the University of Washington in 2020, and completed a Master's Thesis: "An Analysis of Gender Bias in K-12 Assigned Literature Through Comparison of Non-Contextual Word Embedding Models". I graduated from the University of Michigan in 2017 with a dual major in Computer Science and Cognitive Science.


Outside of work, I make music, work on mechanical keyboards, write fiction, run TTRPGS, and play video games. You can find out more about me using the links above.

Hey there. I'm a computer scientist, cognitive scientist, linguist, heavy metal enthusiast, and musician. I graduated from the University of Michigan in April 2017, and I work as a Software Engineer at Microsoft on the Cortana Music team. I'm also a Master's student at the Univeristy of Washington studying Computational Linguistics.


I try to stay involved and engaged in many things. My primary interests are Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, Computational Creativity, User Experience/Interfaces, Transparency and Ethics, Public Policy, Civic Technology, Education Technology, and Technology for Social Good.

  • I'm a member of the IEEE Global Initiative for Ethical Considerations in Artificial Intelligence where I contribute to the next version of the Ethically Aligned Design document as a member of the Mixed Reality and Affective Computing groups, the IEEE P7000 Working Group: Model Process for Addressing Ethical Concerns During System Design, and the IEEE P7001 Working Group: Transparency of Autonomous Systems. I love technology policy, and I want to have a voice in making sure Artificial Intelligence is ethical, human, and transparent.


Wobetto: A simple social platform that helps you discover opportunities to collaborate with students nearby. This project uses a Parse backend with an HTML5/CSS3/JavaScript front-end. I did the entirety of the landing site from scratch, and the majority of the UI for the main app. This startup failed, but we learned a lot. We were selected as members of the Ann Arbor SPARK Startup Bootcamp, Class of Fall 2015.(Co-Founder, CMO, Lead UI Dev/Designer)


SpaceBox: A space-themed pure CSS3 animation. Some JS was used to procedurally generate stars and blinking intervals in the background. The shadows on the moon as it orbits proved to be challenging- this was a fun project nonetheless! I reused the blinking stars on this site.
