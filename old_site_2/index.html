  • I'm a member of the IEEE Global Initiative for Ethical Considerations in Artificial Intelligence where I contribute to the next version of the Ethically Aligned Design document as a member of the Mixed Reality and Affective Computing groups, the IEEE P7000 Working Group: Model Process for Addressing Ethical Concerns During System Design, and the IEEE P7001 Working Group: Transparency of Autonomous Systems. Wobetto: A simple social platform that helps you discover opportunities to collaborate with students nearby. This project uses a Parse backend with an HTML5/CSS3/JavaScript front-end. I did the entirety of the landing site from scratch, and the majority of the UI for the main app. This startup failed, but we learned a lot. We were selected as members of the Ann Arbor SPARK Startup Bootcamp, Class of Fall 2015.(Co-Founder, CMO, Lead UI Dev/Designer)


SpaceBox: A space-themed pure CSS3 animation. Some JS was used to procedurally generate stars and blinking intervals in the background. The shadows on the moon as it orbits proved to be challenging- this was a fun project nonetheless! I reused the blinking stars on this site.
